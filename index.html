Sandbox Manual & Guide

1. Theory Palette (Audition & Inject)

The palette automatically generates valid diatonic chords based on your Global Key & Scale settings.

  • Audition: Tap the main body of any chord block to monitor its voicing without timeline injection.
  • Add to Timeline: Tap the green + ADD bar to append the structure to your sequencer.

2. Arrangement Timeline (The Sequencer)

Every instantiated block features 4 programmable parameters:

  • OCT (Octave): Transposes the fundamental frequency.
  • TYPE (Quality): Alters harmonic intervals (e.g., transitioning Major to Minor 9th).
  • INV (Inversion): Re-orders the stack, pivoting the lowest note an octave higher for superior Voice Leading.
  • DUR (Duration): Dictates quantization. 1m = Full Measure. 2n = Half Note. 4n = Quarter Note.

3. Left Hand & Arp Generators

These modules systematically calculate complex MIDI data driven by your chord definitions.

  • Left Hand: "Root Block" executes sustained fundamentals. "Walking" algorithmically computes diatonic passing intervals. Utilise the Octave dropdown to shift the entire bass progression.
  • Arp Mode: Deconstructs block chords into quantized 16th-note rhythmic sequences.

4. Dual-Bus Mixer & DSP

The architecture implements discrete signal paths for low and high frequencies.

  • Bass Bus: Maintained strictly mono to eliminate phase cancellation.
  • Chord Bus: Processed via the Master FX chain (Ping-Pong Delay, Hall Reverb, LPF Cutoff) for maximum stereo width.
  • Master Limit: A brick-wall limiter acts as the final node, guaranteeing zero digital clipping (> 0dBFS).
